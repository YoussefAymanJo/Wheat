Wheat Genes Project — About Us

A multidisciplinary team bringing together domain experts, active researchers, and software developers to accelerate wheat genetics research and applied solutions.

Mission: To catalog, analyse and share high-quality genetic information about wheat to support breeding, disease resistance research, and global food security.

Senior agronomists, geneticists and plant pathologists who validate domain knowledge, advise on experiment design, and review findings for real-world relevance.
